Hazardous Algae Removal System

May 2023 – Aug 2023

Fluid MechanicsSolar-powered System 3D PrintingRisk & Validation Testing

Overview

Designed a solar-powered system to mitigate harmful algae blooms by circulating cooler water from 13–15m depths. Applied fluid mechanics to optimize flow, tested prototypes via 3D printing, and conducted risk and validation testing. Presented a project poster to students, faculty, and visitors at a public showcase.

What I Did

  • Designed the system concept and flow path to bring cooler deep water upward.
  • Applied fluid mechanics principles to optimize flow and reduce losses.
  • Built and tested prototypes via 3D printing; iterated based on test findings.
  • Performed risk assessment and validation testing to support design decisions.

Communication

  • Presented a project poster at a public showcase for students, faculty, and visitors.
